<p><p>An inorganic photosensitive drum is a key component of laser printers and photocopiers, responsible for capturing and transferring images onto paper. These drums are made of materials such as selenium, silicon, and germanium, which are photoconductive and allow for the formation of electrostatic images. Inorganic photosensitive drums offer high durability, excellent image quality, and are capable of handling high-speed printing.</p><p>The inorganic photosensitive drum market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period.
