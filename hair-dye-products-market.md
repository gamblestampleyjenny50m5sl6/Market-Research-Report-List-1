<p><p>Hair dye products market offers three main types to consumers: temporary hair dye, semi-permanent hair dye, and permanent hair dye. Temporary hair dye lasts only a few washes, perfect for short-term color changes. Semi-permanent hair dye lasts longer, typically up to a few weeks. Permanent hair dye, on the other hand, provides a lasting color change until new hair growth occurs. Each type offers different levels of commitment and flexibility for consumers seeking to change or enhance their hair color.</p></p>
