Transparent vinyl films provide clarity and visibility, making them ideal for showcasing images or graphics. Opaque vinyl films block out light and provide privacy, often used for covering windows or creating solid backgrounds. Each type of vinyl film serves different purposes and caters to specific needs in various industries such as advertising, signage, and decoration.</p></p>
