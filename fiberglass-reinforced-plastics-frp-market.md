<p><p>Fiberglass Reinforced Plastics (FRP) are composite materials made of a polymer matrix reinforced with fibers of glass. The market can be divided into two types - Thermosetting Plastic Products and Thermoplastic Plastic Products. Thermosetting products are hardened through irreversible chemical changes, resulting in a rigid structure. Thermoplastic products can be heated and reformed multiple times without losing their properties. Both types of FRP products are widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, and aerospace for their strength, durability, and lightweight properties.</p></p>
