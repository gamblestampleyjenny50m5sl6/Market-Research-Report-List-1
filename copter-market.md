<p><p>Copter market types include single-rotor type, coaxial twin rotor, and split shaft twin rotor designs. Single-rotor types have a larger main rotor and tail rotor for stability. Coaxial twin rotor models have two rotors mounted on top of each other to cancel out torque and provide stability. Split shaft twin rotor designs have two rotors on separate shafts for improved maneuverability. Each type caters to different flying needs and preferences, offering a range of options for enthusiasts and professionals in the drone industry.</p></p>
