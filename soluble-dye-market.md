<p><p>Soluble dye markets can be divided into two main types: oil soluble and alcohol solubility. Oil soluble dyes are designed to dissolve in oil-based products, making them suitable for use in cosmetics, plastics, and oils. On the other hand, alcohol soluble dyes dissolve easily in alcohol-based products, commonly used in the production of inks, paints, and coatings. Both types of dyes offer versatility in various industries and applications due to their ability to dissolve effectively in specific solvents.</p></p>
