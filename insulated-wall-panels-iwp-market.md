There are two main types of Insulated Wall Panels in the market: PU Insulation Panels and XPS Insulation Panels. PU Insulation Panels are made of polyurethane foam and provide excellent thermal insulation properties. XPS Insulation Panels, on the other hand, are made of extruded polystyrene foam and offer high compressive strength and moisture resistance. Both types of Insulated Wall Panels are popular choices for commercial and residential buildings.</p></p>
