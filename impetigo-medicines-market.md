<p><p>Impetigo is a highly contagious bacterial skin infection characterized by red sores on the face, especially around the nose, mouth, and hands. Impetigo medicines are pharmaceutical drugs used to treat this condition, typically in the form of topical antibiotics such as mupirocin or oral antibiotics like dicloxacillin or erythromycin. These medicines help to eliminate the bacteria causing the infection and promote healing.</p><p>The global market for impetigo medicines has been witnessing steady growth in recent years. Factors such as the increasing prevalence of impetigo cases, rising awareness about the condition, and advancements in healthcare infrastructure contribute to the market growth. Additionally, the growing adoption of topical medicines and the development of new treatment options further drive the market expansion.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing various trends that are shaping its future. One such trend is the increasing preference for combination therapies, where multiple antibiotics are used in a single formulation.
