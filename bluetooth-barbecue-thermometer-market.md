<p><ul><li>Dual Probe</li><li>Single Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bluetooth barbecue thermometers come in two main types: dual probe and single probe. Dual probe thermometers allow for monitoring the temperature of two different items simultaneously, making them ideal for grilling multiple dishes at once. Single probe thermometers, on the other hand, are designed for monitoring the temperature of a single item, making them more suitable for smaller grilling tasks or for those who do not need to monitor multiple items at once. Both types offer the convenience of being able to monitor cooking temperatures remotely via a Bluetooth connection.</p></p>
