<p><p>Cervical orthosis refers to neck braces used for immobilization and support in neck injuries or conditions. The market consists of two main types: soft brace and rigid brace. Soft braces are made of comfortable and flexible materials, providing mild to moderate support. Rigid braces, on the other hand, are made of sturdy materials like plastic or metal, providing firm support and limiting neck movement. Soft braces are usually recommended for minor neck injuries or after surgery, while rigid braces are more suitable for severe injuries or diseases like cervical spondylosis.</p></p>
