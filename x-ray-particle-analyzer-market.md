<p><p>X-ray Particle Analyzers are available in two main types: X-ray Diffraction Type and X-ray Fluorescence Type. X-ray Diffraction Type analyzers use X-rays to determine the crystal structure of a material, providing information on its composition and phase. On the other hand, X-ray Fluorescence Type analyzers detect the characteristic X-ray emissions of a sample, allowing for elemental analysis. Both types of analyzers are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, mining, and environmental monitoring for material identification and quality control.</p></p>
