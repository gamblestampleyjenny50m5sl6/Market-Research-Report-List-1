<p><p>Prefilled disposable systems are categorized into two main types: simple chamber and dual chamber systems. Simple chamber systems consist of a single compartment for holding the medication, while dual chamber systems have two separate compartments for storing different components that need to be mixed before administration. The simple chamber system is commonly used for single-drug formulations, while the dual chamber system is preferred for medications that need to be kept separate until they are ready to be used.</p></p>
