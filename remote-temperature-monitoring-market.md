<p><p>Remote Temperature Monitoring Market refers to the industry that deals with monitoring and measuring temperature remotely. Two main types of temperature sensors are utilized in this market: non-contact temperature sensors and contact temperature sensors. Non-contact temperature sensors make use of infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance, without physically touching the object being measured. On the other hand, contact temperature sensors require direct physical contact with the object to measure its temperature accurately. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries that require continuous temperature monitoring for safety and quality control purposes.</p></p>
