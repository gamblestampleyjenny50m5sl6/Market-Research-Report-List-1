<p><p>The toy drones market can be categorized into three main types: fixed wing, rotor, and flapping wing drones. Fixed wing drones are more similar to traditional aircraft, with wings that generate lift as they move forward. Rotor drones, on the other hand, use multiple rotors to achieve lift and propulsion, allowing for more agile movements. Flapping wing drones mimic the flight patterns of birds and insects, offering unique maneuverability and efficiency. Each type offers different features and capabilities for users to enjoy.</p></p>
