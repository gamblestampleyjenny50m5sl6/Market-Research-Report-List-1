<p><p>The HDPE Blow Molded Products Market comprises two main types of manufacturing processes: Blow Molding and Injection Molding. Blow molding is a process in which melted HDPE (high-density polyethylene) is expanded to form a hollow shape by blowing air into it. This method is commonly used for producing bottles, containers, and automotive components. On the other hand, injection molding involves injecting molten HDPE into a mold cavity, creating solid shapes like caps, closures, and household items. Both technologies play a significant role in manufacturing a wide range of HDPE products.</p></p>
