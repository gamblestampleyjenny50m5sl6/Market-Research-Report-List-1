<p><p>The kidney dialysis equipment market includes various applications, namely: Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD), Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD), Intermittent Hemodialysis (IHD), Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy (CRRT), and Sustained Low Efficiency Dialysis (SLED). APD is a machine-assisted dialysis where the peritoneal cavity filters waste from the blood. CAPD is a manual process where patients perform dialysis themselves without machines. IHD involves removing and filtering blood through a machine. CRRT is a slow and continuous process used in critically ill patients. SLED is a modification of CRRT that provides short and intense sessions. These applications are vital in assisting patients with kidney dysfunction in filtering waste and maintaining their health.</p></p>
