<p><ul><li>Fully Automated</li><li>Semi Automated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor stand biochemical analyzer market is categorized into two types: fully automated and semi-automated. A fully automated analyzer is designed to perform all steps of biochemical analysis automatically, from sample handling to result reporting. It requires minimal human intervention and allows for high throughput of samples. On the other hand, a semi-automated analyzer partially automates the analysis process, requiring some manual steps by the operator. While it can handle a lower volume of samples compared to a fully automated system, it is often more affordable and suitable for smaller laboratories or clinics.</p></p>
