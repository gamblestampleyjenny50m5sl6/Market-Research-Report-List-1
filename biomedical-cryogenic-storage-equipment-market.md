<p><p>The biomedical cryogenic storage equipment market includes various types of equipment used for storing medical samples at different temperatures. Medical refrigerators maintain temperatures between 2℃ and 8℃, suitable for storing medications and vaccines. Blood refrigerators are designed to keep blood samples at 4℃ with a tolerance of ±1℃. Medical freezers maintain temperatures between -10℃ and -25℃ for preserving biological materials. Medical cryopreservation boxes can store samples at temperatures ranging from -10℃ to -50℃. Medical cryogenic storage boxes are capable of storing samples between -50℃ and -150℃. Liquid nitrogen tanks enable extremely low temperatures, ranging from -150℃ to -196℃, suitable for long-term storage of biological materials.</p></p>
