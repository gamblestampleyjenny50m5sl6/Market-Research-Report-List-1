<p><p>In-Display Fingerprint Scanner Market Types: Optical, Ultrasonic, Capacitive. Optical scanners use light to capture the fingerprint image, Ultrasonic scanners use sound waves to create a 3D image of the fingerprint, and Capacitive scanners use electrical currents to detect fingerprint patterns. Each type has its own advantages and drawbacks in terms of accuracy, speed, and security. Optical scanners are typically faster but less secure, Ultrasonic scanners offer high security but may be slower, while Capacitive scanners strike a balance between speed and security.</p></p>
