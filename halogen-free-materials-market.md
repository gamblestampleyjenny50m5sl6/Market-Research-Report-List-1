<p><p>Halogen Free Materials Market includes various types such as TPU Halogen Free Materials, PPO Halogen Free Materials, and TPE (Except TPU) Halogen Free Materials. TPU Halogen Free Materials are thermoplastic polyurethanes that do not contain halogenated flame retardants. PPO Halogen Free Materials are polyphenylene oxide materials that do not contain halogens. TPE (Except TPU) Halogen Free Materials are thermoplastic elastomers that do not contain halogens. These materials are preferred in industries where halogen-free properties are required for safety and environmental reasons.</p></p>
