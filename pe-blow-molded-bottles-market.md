<p><p>PE Blow Molded Bottles Market primarily consists of two types of materials - High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE bottles are known for their strong, durable and chemical-resistant properties, making them ideal for packaging liquids like chemicals, detergents, and oils. On the other hand, LDPE bottles are more flexible and are commonly used for packaging products like lotions, shampoos, and food products. Both HDPE and LDPE bottles are widely used in various industries due to their versatility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
