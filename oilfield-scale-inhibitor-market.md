<p><p>Oilfield scale inhibitors are chemicals used to prevent the formation of scales in oil production equipment. The market types include Phosphonates, Carboxylate/Acrylate, Sulfonates, and Others. Phosphonates are effective in controlling calcium carbonate and calcium sulfate scales. Carboxylate/Acrylate inhibitors are used to control calcium carbonate, calcium sulfate, and barium sulfate scales. Sulfonates are effective in controlling calcium sulfate scales. Other inhibitors include polymeric inhibitors and phosphate-based inhibitors that target specific scales in oilfield equipment.</p></p>
