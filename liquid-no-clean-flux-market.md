<p><strong>The Liquid No-Clean Flux Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Residue</li><li>Extremely Low Residue</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid no-clean flux is available in different types based on the level of residue they leave on the circuit board after soldering. Low residue flux leaves a minimal amount of residue, making it suitable for applications where residue tolerance is limited. Extremely low residue flux leaves almost no residue, perfect for applications where cleanliness is critical, such as in medical devices or aerospace electronics. Both types offer the benefit of not requiring cleaning after soldering, saving time and resources in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
