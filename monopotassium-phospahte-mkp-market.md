<p><p>Monopotassium Phosphate (MKP) is a water-soluble fertilizer that is available in three main market types: powder, crystal, and liquid. Powder MKP is a dry form that can be easily stored and handled. Crystal MKP is a solid form that dissolves quickly in water for easy application. Liquid MKP is a ready-to-use solution that can be directly sprayed onto crops. These different market types provide flexibility for farmers in choosing the most convenient form of MKP for their agricultural needs.</p></p>
