<p><p>Feed additives for ruminants can be categorized into two main types: dry feed additives and liquid feed additives. Dry feed additives are typically in powder or pellet form and are easily mixed into the animal's dry feed. Liquid feed additives, on the other hand, are liquid solutions or emulsions that can be added to the animal's drinking water or directly onto their feed. Both types of additives serve the purpose of improving the nutritional quality of the animal's diet and enhancing their overall health and performance.</p></p>
