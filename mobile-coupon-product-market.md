<p><p>Mobile coupon products can be categorized into three main market types: SMS transiver, readable codes or tags, and NFC devices. SMS transiver enables coupons to be sent directly to a mobile device via text message. Readable codes or tags consist of unique codes or barcodes that can be scanned by a mobile device to redeem the coupon. NFC devices use near field communication technology to transmit coupon information wirelessly to a mobile device when it is in close proximity to the device.</p></p>
