<p><p>Online corporate meeting software market can be categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and hosted on the company's own servers, providing complete control over data and security but requiring maintenance and hardware costs. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on the vendor's servers, allowing users to access and collaborate on meetings remotely with lower upfront costs and automatic updates. It offers scalability and flexibility, but data security concerns may arise due to reliance on external servers.</p></p>
