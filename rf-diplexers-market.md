<p><p>RF Diplexers market can be categorized into three main types: Crystal Type, Ceramics Type, and Others. Crystal Type diplexers use quartz crystals to separate different frequency bands, providing high accuracy and stability. Ceramics Type diplexers utilize ceramic materials for their filtering components, offering a good balance between performance and cost. The Others category includes diplexers that use different technologies such as SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) or LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramics). Each type has its own advantages and applications within the RF industry.</p></p>
