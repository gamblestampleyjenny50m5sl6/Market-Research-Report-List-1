<p><p>The Upholstery Fancy Yarn market consists of various types such as Slub Yarn, Spiral Yarn, Loop Yarn, Snarl Yarn, Chenille Yarn, and Others. Snarl Yarn has twisted and knotted sections. Chenille Yarn is soft and velvety, made from a fuzzy pile protruding from a yarn core. Other types include boucle, nub, and metallic yarns, each with unique characteristics.</p></p>
