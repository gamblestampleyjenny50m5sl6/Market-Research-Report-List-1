<p><p>Proximity card market types can be classified into three categories: Low Frequency Cards, High Frequency Cards, and Ultra-High Frequency Cards. Low Frequency Cards typically operate at frequencies between 125 kHz and are commonly used for access control and time-tracking applications. High Frequency Cards operate at frequencies around 13.56 MHz and are popular for contactless payment systems and ticketing. Ultra-High Frequency Cards operate at frequencies above 800 MHz and are ideal for long-range applications such as vehicle identification and inventory management.</p></p>
