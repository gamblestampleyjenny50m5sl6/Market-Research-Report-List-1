<p><strong>The Surgical 3D Mobile C-Arm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isocentric C-arms</li><li>Non Isocentric C-arms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surgical 3D Mobile C-Arm market can be categorized into two types: Isocentric C-arms and Non-Isocentric C-arms. Isocentric C-arms have a rotational axis that remains at the same point throughout the imaging process, offering precise and accurate imaging in complex surgical procedures. Non-Isocentric C-arms do not have a fixed rotational axis, making them more flexible and suitable for imaging in various positions. Both types play a vital role in surgical interventions, providing surgeons with real-time 3D imaging capabilities to improve accuracy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
