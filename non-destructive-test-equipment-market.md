<p><p>Non-Destructive Test Equipment Market Types consist of different tools and devices used for assessing the quality and integrity of materials without causing damage. Penetration detectors are used to detect surface defects or inconsistencies in materials. Eddy current detectors are used to detect cracks or defects below the surface. Thermometers are used to measure temperature variations which can indicate potential issues. Other types of non-destructive test equipment include ultrasonic testers, visual inspection tools, and magnetic particle detectors. Each type serves a specific purpose in ensuring the safety and quality of materials and structures.</p></p>
