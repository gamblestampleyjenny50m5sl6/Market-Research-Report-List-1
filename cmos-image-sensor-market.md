<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-illuminated (FI) CMOS Sensor</li><li>Backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMOS image sensors are categorized based on their design and structure. Front-illuminated (FI) CMOS sensors have a traditional design where light enters through the front side of the sensor. On the other hand, backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensors have a reverse design where light enters through the backside, allowing for better light sensitivity and improved performance, especially in low-light conditions. BSI sensors are considered more advanced and are gaining popularity in the market for high-quality imaging applications.</p></p>
