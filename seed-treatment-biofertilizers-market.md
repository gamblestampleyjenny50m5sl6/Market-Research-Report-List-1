<p><p>Seed treatment biofertilizers refer to beneficial microorganisms that are applied to seeds to enhance crop growth. The market is categorized into liquid biofertilizers and carrier-based biofertilizers. Liquid biofertilizers are in liquid form and can be easily applied through various methods such as seed soaking and foliar spraying. Carrier-based biofertilizers are solid or granular products that are mixed with seeds or soil before planting. Both types of biofertilizers provide essential nutrients to plants, improve soil health, and increase crop yield.</p></p>
