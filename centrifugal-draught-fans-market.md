<p><p>Centrifugal draught fans are classified into three main types: radial, backward, and forward. Radial fans are designed to produce high pressure and low airflow, making them ideal for applications requiring a higher static pressure. Backward fans are known for their high efficiency and are commonly used in HVAC systems. Forward fans are most suitable for high airflow, low pressure applications. Each type of fan has its own unique design and performance characteristics, making them suitable for a variety of industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
