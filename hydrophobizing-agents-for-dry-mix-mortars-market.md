<p><p>Hydrophobizing agents for dry-mix mortars are used to improve the water repellent properties of the mortar. There are different types of hydrophobic agents available in the market including hydrophobic polymers, silicone-based products, fatty acid type products, and metallic stearates. Hydrophobic polymers are synthetic organic compounds that repel water, while silicone-based products provide excellent water repellency. Fatty acid type products are derived from natural sources and offer good water resistance. Metallic stearates are metal salts of stearic acid that also provide water repellent properties to dry-mix mortars.</p></p>
