<p><p>The oil and gas water treatment chemicals market includes various types of chemicals used to treat water in the oil and gas industry. Biocides and disinfectants are substances that kill or inhibit the growth of microorganisms. Defoaming agents prevent the formation of foam in water used for oil and gas operations. Scale and corrosion inhibitors prevent the buildup of mineral deposits and protect metal surfaces. Oxygen scavengers remove dissolved oxygen from water. Flocculants and coagulants help in removing solid particles from water. Demulsifiers separate water from oil emulsions.</p></p>
