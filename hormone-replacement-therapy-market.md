<p><p>Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT) is a medical treatment that involves the use of synthetic hormones to supplement the declining levels of hormones in the body, particularly estrogen and progesterone in women and testosterone in men. HRT is commonly used to alleviate symptoms associated with menopause in women, such as hot flashes, night sweats, mood swings, and vaginal dryness. It can also be used to manage symptoms related to testosterone deficiency in men, including fatigue, decreased libido, and muscle weakness.</p><p>The hormone replacement therapy market is expected to experience substantial growth in the coming years. The increasing prevalence of hormonal disorders, such as menopause and testosterone deficiency, is driving the demand for hormone replacement therapy. Additionally, the rising geriatric population worldwide, coupled with growing awareness about the benefits of HRT, is contributing to market growth.</p><p>Advancements in medical technology and the development of innovative hormone replacement therapy products are also spurring market growth.
