<p><p>The Oilfield Fracturing Chemicals Market includes various types of chemicals such as clear brine fluids, shale inhibitors, lubricants, non-emulsifiers, H2S scavengers, defoamers, and surfactants. Clear brine fluids are used to control well pressure during drilling operations, while shale inhibitors help prevent the swelling of shale formations. Lubricants reduce friction in the wellbore, non-emulsifiers help separate oil and water, H2S scavengers remove hydrogen sulfide gas, defoamers eliminate foam, and surfactants aid in fluid flow and production efficiency.</p></p>
