<p><strong>The Pool Salt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rock Salt</li><li>Solar Salt</li><li>Evap Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pool salt is available in three main types: rock salt, solar salt, and evap salt. Rock salt is mined from underground salt deposits and has larger crystals which dissolve slower. Solar salt is produced by evaporating seawater using the sun, resulting in fine crystals that dissolve quickly. Evap salt is made by mechanically evaporating brine to produce fine, pure crystals. Each type of salt has its own unique characteristics that cater to different preferences and conditions for pool owners.</p></p>
