Fixed wing machine simulation involves training for fixed-wing aircraft such as fighter jets and transport planes, emphasizing aerial combat tactics and navigation. UAV simulation involves training operators for remotely piloted aircraft, offering practice in mission planning, control, and surveillance operations.
