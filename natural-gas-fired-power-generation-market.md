<p><strong>The Natural Gas-Fired Power Generation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCGT</li><li>OCGT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural gas-fired power generation includes both Combined Cycle Gas Turbine (CCGT) and Open Cycle Gas Turbine (OCGT) technologies. CCGT plants are more efficient and cost-effective, as they utilize waste heat to generate additional power. They are often used for baseload power generation due to their continuous operation. OCGT plants, on the other hand, are more flexible and can quickly ramp up or down in response to demand fluctuations. They are commonly used for peaking power generation during periods of high electricity demand.</p></p>
