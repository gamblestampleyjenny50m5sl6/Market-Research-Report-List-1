<p><ul><li>Up to 70 GSM</li><li>70 GSM to 90 GSM</li><li>90 GSM to 120 GSM</li><li>Above 120 GSM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct thermal tickets are categorized into four market types based on their GSM (Grams per Square Meter) weight - Up to 70 GSM, 70 GSM to 90 GSM, 90 GSM to 120 GSM, and Above 120 GSM. Up to 70 GSM tickets are lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for applications where durability is not a primary concern. 70 GSM to 90 GSM tickets offer a balance between cost and durability. 90 GSM to 120 GSM tickets provide higher durability, ideal for applications with higher wear and tear. Above 120 GSM tickets are the most durable and heavy-duty option, suitable for long-lasting applications.</p></p>
