<p><strong>The Anti-Corrosion Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Anti Corrosion Oils</li><li>Water-based Anti Corrosion Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-corrosion oils are classified into two main types based on their composition: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based anti-corrosion oils are formulated with organic solvents that evaporate after application, leaving behind a protective film on the surface. These oils are ideal for applications where water cannot be used. On the other hand, water-based anti-corrosion oils are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. They are preferred for applications where water can be used safely. Both types of anti-corrosion oils provide effective protection against corrosion.</p></p>
