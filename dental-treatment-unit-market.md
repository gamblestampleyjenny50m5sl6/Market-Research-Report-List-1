<p><p>A Dental Treatment Unit refers to a combination of various dental equipment and instruments used by dentists during dental procedures. There are two main types of Dental Treatment Units: With Chair and Without Chair. With Chair units consist of a dental chair along with all the necessary equipment like air compressors, suction pumps, and water supply systems. These units provide complete functionality for a dentist to perform dental procedures. On the other hand, Without Chair units do not include a dental chair and are designed to be used with existing dental chairs, providing only the essential equipment for dental procedures.</p></p>
