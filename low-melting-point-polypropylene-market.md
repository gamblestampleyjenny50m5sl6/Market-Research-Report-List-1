<p><p>Low melting point polypropylene is categorized into different market types based on their melt flow rate (MFR). The MFR is measured in grams per 10 minutes and indicates the rate at which the polymer can be processed. The 0-1 g/10 min market type consists of polypropylene with very low MFR, making it suitable for applications requiring high mechanical strength. The 1.1-3.5 g/10 min market type is commonly used for injection molding applications. The 3.6-5 g/10 min market type is ideal for extrusion processes due to its slightly higher MFR.</p></p>
