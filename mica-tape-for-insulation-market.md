<p><strong>The Mica Tape for Insulation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mica Glass Tape</li><li>Mica Polyester Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mica tape for insulation is available in two main types: Mica Glass Tape and Mica Polyester Tape. Mica Glass Tape is made of high-quality mica paper combined with glass cloth for enhanced strength and durability. It is commonly used in high-temperature applications due to its excellent thermal resistance. On the other hand, Mica Polyester Tape is made of mica paper combined with a polyester film for flexibility and ease of handling. It is often used in electrical insulation applications where flexibility is a key requirement.</p></p>
