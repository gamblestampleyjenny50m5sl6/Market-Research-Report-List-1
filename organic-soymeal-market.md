<p><p>Organic soymeal can be categorized into two main types - full-fat soymeal and defatted soymeal. Full-fat soymeal, as the name suggests, contains a higher amount of oil, offering a rich source of energy for livestock feed. On the other hand, defatted soymeal has had a portion of its oil content removed, making it lower in fat but higher in protein. Both types of organic soymeal are popular choices in the animal feed industry for their nutritional benefits and versatility in various livestock diets.</p></p>
