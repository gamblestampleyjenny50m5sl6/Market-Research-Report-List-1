<p><p>Commercial amphibious vehicles can be categorized into four main market types: Screw Propeller, Water Jet, Track-based, and Others. Vehicles equipped with screw propellers are driven forward by rotating screws, while water jet vehicles use high-pressure water jets for propulsion. Track-based vehicles utilize tank-like tracks for movement on both land and water. The "Others" category includes vehicles with unique propulsion systems such as air cushions or hybrid designs. Each market type offers different strengths and capabilities for various commercial applications.</p></p>
