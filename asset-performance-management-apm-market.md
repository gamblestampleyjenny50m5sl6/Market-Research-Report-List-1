<p><p>Asset Performance Management (APM) market is categorized into Asset Integrity Management and Asset Reliability Management. Asset Integrity Management focuses on ensuring assets are operating safely and efficiently throughout their lifecycle. It involves monitoring, inspection, maintenance, and risk management to prevent failures. On the other hand, Asset Reliability Management aims to optimize asset performance by maximizing equipment uptime, reducing maintenance costs, and improving overall operational efficiency. Both markets play crucial roles in ensuring the longevity and effectiveness of assets in various industries.</p></p>
