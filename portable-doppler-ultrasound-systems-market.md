<p><strong>The Portable Doppler Ultrasound Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Directional Doppler</li><li>Non-Directional Doppler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Directional Doppler systems are portable ultrasound devices used to measure the velocity and direction of blood flow within the body. They are commonly used in vascular surgery and cardiology. Non-directional Doppler systems, on the other hand, are also portable ultrasound devices but only measure the presence of blood flow, without providing information on the direction or velocity. They are utilized in various medical fields including obstetrics and gynecology for monitoring fetal heart rates. Both types of systems offer convenient and efficient diagnostic capabilities in a portable format.</p></p>
