<p><p>Modular precast construction products are divided into various types based on their specific function and usage. Columns & beams are used to support structures, while floors & roofs provide the base and cover for buildings. Girders are horizontal supports that help distribute weight evenly. Walls are essential for enclosing and dividing spaces, while staircases provide vertical circulation. Lintels are used to support the load over openings like doors and windows. Paving slabs are used for outdoor flooring purposes. Each of these products plays a crucial role in constructing modular precast buildings.</p></p>
