<p><p>The natural fertilizer market is divided into three main types: peat-based, manure-based, and others. Peat-based fertilizers are made from decomposed organic matter found in peat bogs, providing a rich source of nutrients for plants. Manure-based fertilizers are derived from animal waste, offering a natural and sustainable option for soil enrichment. Other types of natural fertilizers include seaweed-based, bone meal, and compost, with each offering unique benefits for plant growth and soil health. These options cater to the growing demand for environmentally-friendly and organic agricultural practices.</p></p>
