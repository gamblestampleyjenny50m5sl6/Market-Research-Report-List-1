As consumers seek affordable and aesthetically pleasing furniture, manufacturers are increasingly using MDF as a substitute for solid wood due to its lower cost and improved design possibilities.</p><p>Additionally, the construction industry plays a crucial role in the growth of the MDF market. MDF is widely used for interior applications such as doors, cabinets, molding, and flooring due to its stability and durability. The booming construction sector in emerging economies and the growing focus on sustainable construction practices are anticipated to boost the demand for MDF in the coming years.</p><p>Furthermore, the packaging industry has also been a significant end-user of MDF.
