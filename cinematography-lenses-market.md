<p><strong>The Cinematography Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prime Lenses</li><li>Zoom Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cinematography lenses market is divided into two main types - Prime Lenses and Zoom Lenses. Prime Lenses have a fixed focal length, providing superior image quality and low light performance. They are ideal for capturing high-quality, sharp images with a shallow depth of field. On the other hand, Zoom Lenses offer versatility with their adjustable focal lengths, allowing cinematographers to easily change the framing of their shots without having to physically move the camera. Both types cater to different needs in the filmmaking industry.</p></p>
