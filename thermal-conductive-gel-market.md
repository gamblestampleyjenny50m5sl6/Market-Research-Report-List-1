<p><p>Thermal conductive gel is used in various industries for heat dissipation purposes. One component thermal conductive gel is premixed and ready to use, simplifying the application process. On the other hand, two component thermal conductive gel requires mixing of two separate components before application, offering more control over the final properties of the gel. Both types of gels provide efficient thermal conductivity and are used in electronics, automotive, and other high-heat applications to improve heat transfer and prevent overheating.</p></p>
