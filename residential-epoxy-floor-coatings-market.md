<p><strong>The Residential Epoxy Floor Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>100% Solids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential epoxy floor coatings are categorized into three main types based on their composition: water-based, solvent-based, and 100% solids. Water-based coatings are known for their low odor and ease of application, making them popular among homeowners. Solvent-based coatings offer more durability and chemical resistance but come with a stronger odor during application. 100% solids coatings are the most durable option, providing a thick finish that is resistant to wear and abrasions. Each type has its own advantages and suitability based on different needs and preferences.</p></p>
