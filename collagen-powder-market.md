<p><p>The collagen powder market can be categorized into three types: gelatin, hydrolyzed collagen, and others. Gelatin is derived from animal sources and is commonly used for baking and cooking purposes. Hydrolyzed collagen, also known as collagen peptides, undergoes a process that breaks down the collagen into smaller particles, making it more easily absorbed by the body. It is widely used in the cosmetic and nutraceutical industries. The "others" category includes collagen powders derived from alternative sources, such as marine collagen or plant-based collagen substitutes.</p></p>
