<p><p>Dishwashing detergent market types can be categorized into saponification and non-saponification. Saponification detergents are made through a chemical reaction involving oils and lye, resulting in soap formation. These detergents are known for their ability to effectively clean dishes and cut through grease. On the other hand, non-saponification detergents use synthetic surfactants to clean dishes. While both types serve the same purpose, saponification detergents are often preferred for their eco-friendly and natural ingredients.</p></p>
