<p><ul><li>Magnesium die-casting</li><li>Magnesium sand-casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnesium die-casting involves injecting molten magnesium into a mold under high pressure, resulting in intricate and complex shapes with tight tolerances. This method is commonly used for mass production of components such as automotive parts and electronic devices. On the other hand, magnesium sand-casting involves pouring molten magnesium into a sand mold, which is a cost-effective method for producing simple shapes and prototypes. Both methods have their advantages and are utilized in various industries based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
