<p><strong>The Thermally Conductive Battery Adhesive for Hybrid Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gap Fillers</li><li>Structural Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermally conductive battery adhesive for hybrid vehicles serves two main market types: Gap Fillers and Structural Adhesives. Gap fillers are used to fill voids and gaps between battery cells, ensuring optimal thermal conductivity and heat dissipation. Structural adhesives, on the other hand, provide mechanical bonding between different components of the battery pack, enhancing the overall stability and durability of the system. Both types of adhesives play a crucial role in maximizing the performance and lifespan of batteries in hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
