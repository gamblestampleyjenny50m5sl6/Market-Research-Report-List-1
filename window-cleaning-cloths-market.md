<p><p>Window cleaning cloths come in two main types: multi-component microfiber cloths and mono-component microfiber cloths. Multi-component microfiber cloths are made up of multiple materials woven together, resulting in a highly absorbent and effective cleaning cloth. On the other hand, mono-component microfiber cloths are made of a single material, providing a simpler but still efficient option for window cleaning. Both types of cloths are popular choices in the market for their ability to effectively clean windows without streaks or residue.</p></p>
