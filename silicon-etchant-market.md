<p><ul><li>Potassium Hydroxide Type</li><li>Hydrofluoric Acid/Acetic Acid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Etchant market is segmented into two main types: Potassium Hydroxide Type and Hydrofluoric Acid/Acetic Acid Type. The Potassium Hydroxide Type involves the use of potassium hydroxide as the primary etchant for silicon wafers, offering high selectivity and stability. On the other hand, the Hydrofluoric Acid/Acetic Acid Type utilizes a mixture of hydrofluoric acid and acetic acid for etching silicon, providing a high etch rate and smooth surface finish. Both types cater to different industry needs and applications within the Silicon Etchant market.</p></p>
