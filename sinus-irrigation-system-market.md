<p><p>The sinus irrigation system market consists of two main types: manual sinus irrigation systems and electric sinus irrigation systems. Manual sinus irrigation systems require users to manually control the flow of saline solution into their sinuses through a Neti pot or squeeze bottle. Electric sinus irrigation systems, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and use a motorized pump to deliver the saline solution into the sinuses. These systems automate the process, offering convenience and potentially more effective results.</p></p>
