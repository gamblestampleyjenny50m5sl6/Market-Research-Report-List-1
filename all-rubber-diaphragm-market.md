<p><p>Rubber diaphragms are used in various applications and come in different types based on their shape and design. The flat diaphragm type is thin and flexible, suitable for low-pressure applications. Rolling diaphragms have a cylindrical shape, ideal for high-pressure systems. Dish-shaped diaphragms provide a larger surface area for pressure distribution. Convoluted diaphragms have a corrugated design for increased flexibility and durability. Each type of rubber diaphragm serves specific purposes in different industries, offering unique advantages based on their design.</p></p>
