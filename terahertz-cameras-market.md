<p><strong>The Terahertz Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive Terahertz Imaging involves detecting the natural terahertz radiation emitted by objects, allowing for non-intrusive imaging and screening applications such as security checks. Active Terahertz Imaging, on the other hand, involves emitting terahertz waves towards objects and detecting the reflected signals, enabling more precise and detailed imaging in various fields like medical imaging, material testing, and industrial quality control. Both types of Terahertz cameras have their unique advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
