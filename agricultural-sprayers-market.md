<p><p>Agricultural sprayers are classified into three main types based on their application rate: Ultra-Low Volume (ULV), Low Volume (LV), and High Volume (HV) sprayers. ULV sprayers disperse very fine droplets at a low application rate, making them ideal for targeting specific areas with precision. LV sprayers cover a larger area with a moderate application rate, while HV sprayers deliver high volumes of spray over a large area. Farmers choose the appropriate sprayer type based on the specific needs of their crops and fields.</p></p>
