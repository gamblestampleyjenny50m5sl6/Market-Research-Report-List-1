<p><strong>The Industrial Line Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intumescent Wire Coatings</li><li>Non-Intumescent Wire Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Line Coating Market Types include Intumescent Wire Coatings and Non-Intumescent Wire Coatings. Intumescent Wire Coatings are fire-resistant coatings that expand rapidly when exposed to heat, forming a protective insulating char. They are commonly used in buildings and infrastructure to protect against fire damage. On the other hand, Non-Intumescent Wire Coatings are coatings that do not expand upon exposure to heat, but still provide corrosion resistance and protection for wires and cables in industrial settings.</p></p>
