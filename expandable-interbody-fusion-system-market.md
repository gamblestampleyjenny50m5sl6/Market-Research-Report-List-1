<p><p>The Expandable Interbody Fusion System market includes three main types: Lumbar System, Thoraco-lumbar System, and Cervical System. The Lumbar System refers to devices used for interbody fusion in the lower back, specifically the lumbar region. The Thoraco-lumbar System is designed for fusion in the thoracic and lumbar spine, providing stability and support to these areas. The Cervical System is used for fusion in the neck (cervical spine), assisting in the treatment of neck instability and spinal deformities. These systems help in achieving spinal fusion and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
