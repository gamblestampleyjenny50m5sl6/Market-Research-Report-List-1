<p><p>Underwater electrical connectors can be classified into different types based on their design and usage. Dry mate connectors are used in applications where the connection needs to be made and disconnected in a dry environment before being submerged underwater. Wet mate connectors, on the other hand, are designed to be mated underwater to prevent water ingress. Other types of connectors in the market include hybrid connectors that combine features of both dry mate and wet mate connectors for specific application requirements.</p></p>
