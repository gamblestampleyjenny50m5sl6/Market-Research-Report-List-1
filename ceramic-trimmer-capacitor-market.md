<p><ul><li>SMD Ceramic Trimmer Capacitor</li><li>DIP Ceramic Trimmer Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic trimmer capacitors are available in two main types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and DIP (Dual Inline Package). SMD ceramic trimmer capacitors are designed for surface mounting applications, making them ideal for compact and densely packed electronic devices. On the other hand, DIP ceramic trimmer capacitors are suitable for through-hole mounting on PCBs, offering a more traditional approach to circuit design. Both types offer precise tuning capabilities and are widely used in various electronic circuits for frequency adjustment and tuning purposes.</p></p>
