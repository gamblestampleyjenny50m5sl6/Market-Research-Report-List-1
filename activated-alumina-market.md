<p><ul><li>Powdered Form Activated Alumina</li><li>Sphered Form Activated Alumina</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Activated Alumina is available in two main types in the market: Powdered Form and Sphered Form. Powdered Form Activated Alumina is finely ground, increasing its surface area and making it suitable for applications such as water filtration and gas drying. On the other hand, Sphered Form Activated Alumina is shaped into beads or spheres, providing greater strength and lower dust production, making it ideal for use in catalysts and desiccants. Both forms offer unique advantages and find applications in various industries.</p></p>
