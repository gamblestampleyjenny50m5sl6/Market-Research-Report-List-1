<p><p>Diving socks come in two main types: dry diving socks and general diving socks. Dry diving socks are designed to keep the feet dry and warm, usually made of neoprene material with sealed seams. They are ideal for colder water temperatures. General diving socks are made of thinner material and are suitable for warmer waters or as a layer underneath diving fins. Both types of diving socks provide protection from blisters, abrasions, and other foot injuries while diving.</p></p>
