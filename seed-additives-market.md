<p><ul><li>Dry Form</li><li>Liquid Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Seed additives market can be categorized into two types: dry form and liquid form. Dry form seed additives are typically powdered or granular substances that are applied directly to seeds before planting. These additives may include fertilizers, pesticides, or other chemicals to promote growth and protect against pests. On the other hand, liquid form seed additives are typically solutions or suspensions that are applied directly to seeds before planting. These additives are usually more concentrated than their dry form counterparts and may offer more targeted benefits for seed health and growth.</p></p>
