<p><p>Oil-based paints are a type of paint that uses oil as a binder or base material. They are known for their durability, glossy finish, and ability to provide excellent coverage. They have been widely used for centuries in various industries including construction, automotive, and industrial applications.</p><p>The global oil-based paints market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors such as increasing demand for durable and long-lasting paints in the construction industry, rising automotive production, and the growing infrastructure development activities in emerging economies. Manufacturers are focusing on developing eco-friendly oil-based paints by reducing the content of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and harmful chemicals.
