<p><p>Instrument tubes are used in various industries for the transportation of fluids and gases. The two main types of instrument tubes are seamless and welded. Seamless tubes are manufactured without any welding seams, providing a smoother surface and higher corrosion resistance. Welded tubes, on the other hand, are created by welding two pieces of metal together, resulting in a more cost-effective option. Both seamless and welded tubes are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and automotive for their reliability and durability.</p></p>
