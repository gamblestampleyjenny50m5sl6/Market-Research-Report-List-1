<p><p>OLED Display Market consists of two main types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) Display and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) Display. AMOLED displays are preferred for high-end smartphones and TVs due to their faster response times and better color reproduction. PMOLED displays are simpler and less expensive, commonly used in smaller devices like wearable technology and appliances. Both types offer high contrast ratios, thin profiles, and energy efficiency, making them popular choices for a wide range of consumer electronics.</p></p>
