<p><p>The silicone coated release liner market can be classified into two main types based on the placement of the silicone coating: one-sided silicone and double-sided silicone. In one-sided silicone liners, the silicone coating is applied only on one side of the liner, providing a release surface for one side. On the other hand, double-sided silicone liners have silicone coatings on both sides, allowing for dual-sided release properties. Both types cater to different applications and industries based on specific requirements for release capabilities.</p></p>
