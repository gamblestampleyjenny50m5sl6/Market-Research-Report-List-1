<p><strong>The Embedded Buzzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embedded buzzers are commonly used in various electronic devices to generate sound alerts or notifications. Two main types of embedded buzzers are piezoelectric and electromagnetic. Piezoelectric buzzers use a ceramic disc that vibrates when an electric current is applied, producing sound. On the other hand, electromagnetic buzzers use a coil and magnet to create sound waves. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in different applications based on their specific characteristics and requirements.</p></p>
