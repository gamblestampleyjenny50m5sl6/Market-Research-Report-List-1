<p><p>The surgical reusable medical gloves market can be divided into two major types, namely latex gloves and nitrile rubber gloves. Nitrile rubber gloves, on the other hand, are made from synthetic rubber and offer similar performance to latex gloves. They are particularly suitable for individuals with latex allergies and provide excellent puncture resistance and durability.</p></p>
