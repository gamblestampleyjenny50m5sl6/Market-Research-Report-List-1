<p><p>The hotel front desk system market encompasses two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the software through the internet. This eliminates the need for on-site hardware and maintenance, providing flexibility and scalability. On the other hand, on-premises systems require hotels to install the software on their own servers and manage it internally. This offers more control over data but involves higher upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities. Both options cater to different hotel needs, budget constraints, and IT preferences.</p></p>
