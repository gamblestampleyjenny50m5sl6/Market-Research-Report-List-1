<p><p>Metal clad pipes are special types of pipeline systems where a layer of metal is bonded to the outer surface of a core pipe material, usually made of steel or plastic. This method of construction provides the pipes with the strength and durability of metal while also offering the corrosion resistance of the core material. Metal clad pipes are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation.</p><p>The Metal Clad Pipes Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for corrosion-resistant piping solutions in various industries, as well as the benefits offered by metal clad pipes such as improved strength, durability, and longevity. Moreover, the rise in infrastructure development projects, particularly in developing economies, is also driving the demand for metal clad pipes.</p><p>The latest trends in the Metal Clad Pipes Market include advancements in metallurgical technologies leading to the development of more efficient and cost-effective metal cladding techniques, as well as the increasing adoption of metal clad pipes in offshore oil and gas applications due to their superior corrosion resistance properties. Additionally, the growing focus on sustainable and environmentally-friendly piping solutions is also expected to drive the market for metal clad pipes in the coming years.</p></p>
