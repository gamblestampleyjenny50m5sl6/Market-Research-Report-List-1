<p><p>Direct-to-Metal coatings (DTMs) are coatings that can be applied directly to metal surfaces without the need for a primer. In the market, there are two main types of DTMs: Waterborne and Solventborne. Waterborne DTMs are environmentally friendly, as they have lower VOC levels and are easy to clean up with water. Solventborne DTMs, on the other hand, are known for their durability and resistance to harsh conditions. Both types of DTMs offer excellent corrosion protection and adhesion to metal substrates.</p></p>
