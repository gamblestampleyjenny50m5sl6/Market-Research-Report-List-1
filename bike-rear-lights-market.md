<p><p>Bike rear lights are essential for ensuring visibility and safety while cycling at night. There are two main types of bike rear lights on the market: self-generating lights and battery-powered lights. Self-generating lights use the kinetic energy generated from the rotation of the bike's wheels to power the light, making them convenient and eco-friendly. On the other hand, battery-powered lights rely on rechargeable or disposable batteries for power and are known for their long-lasting and bright illumination. Both types offer a reliable lighting solution for cyclists.</p></p>
