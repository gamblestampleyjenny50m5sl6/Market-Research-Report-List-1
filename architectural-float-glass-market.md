<p><p>Clear float glass is a type of architectural glass that is produced using the traditional float glass manufacturing process. It has a greenish tint due to the iron content in the raw materials, but offers good clarity and light transmission. Extra clear float glass, on the other hand, is a high-quality, low-iron glass that is nearly colorless and provides exceptional clarity and transparency. It is often used in applications where clarity and aesthetic appeal are important, such as in facades, windows, and display cases.</p></p>
