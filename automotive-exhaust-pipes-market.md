<p><p>Automotive exhaust pipes come in two main types - single exhaust pipes and dual exhaust pipes. Single exhaust pipes are simpler in design and consist of a single pipe exiting the vehicle. They are commonly found in smaller or more budget-friendly cars. On the other hand, dual exhaust pipes have two separate pipes exiting the vehicle, typically seen in high-performance vehicles. They are known for offering improved exhaust flow and can provide a sportier sound. Both types serve the purpose of directing exhaust gases away from the engine and reducing emissions.</p></p>
