<p><p>Aluminum Base PCBs are categorized into three main types: Single-sided Aluminum Base PCB, Double-sided Aluminum Base PCB, and Aluminum Base Multilayer PCB. Single-sided Aluminum Base PCBs have a single layer of conductive material on one side, while Double-sided Aluminum Base PCBs have two layers of conductive material on both sides. Aluminum Base Multilayer PCBs consist of multiple layers of conductive material sandwiched between aluminum layers. Each type of Aluminum Base PCB offers varying levels of complexity and functionality for different electronic applications.</p></p>
