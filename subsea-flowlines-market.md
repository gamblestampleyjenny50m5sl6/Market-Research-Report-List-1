<p><p>Subsea flowlines are categorized into low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure markets based on the level of pressure they can withstand. Low pressure flowlines are designed for systems operating at pressures below 1,500 psi, medium pressure flowlines can handle pressures up to 3,000 psi, while high pressure flowlines are capable of withstanding pressures greater than 3,000 psi. These different types cater to specific needs and applications in the offshore oil and gas industry, ensuring safe and efficient transport of fluids under varying pressure conditions.</p></p>
