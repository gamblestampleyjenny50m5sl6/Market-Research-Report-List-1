<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Styrene acrylic resins are widely used in the market in two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based varieties are preferred for their eco-friendly and low-VOC properties, making them suitable for applications in industries with strict environmental regulations. On the other hand, solvent-based resins offer faster drying times and superior adhesion properties, making them ideal for demanding applications such as automotive coatings. Both types of resins are favored for their high flexibility, durability, and weather resistance, catering to a wide range of industries and applications.</p></p>
