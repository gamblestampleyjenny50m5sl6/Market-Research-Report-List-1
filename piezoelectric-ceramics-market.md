<p><p>Piezoelectric ceramics market can be categorized into various types based on composition and properties. Some of the major types include Lead zinc titanates (PZT), Lead titanate (PT), Lead magnesium niobate (PMN), and others. PZT is widely used due to its high piezoelectric constant and strong electromechanical coupling. PT exhibits high electromechanical coupling coefficient and low dielectric constant. PMN is known for its high piezoelectric constant and large strain capabilities. Other types in the market offer unique properties and are utilized in niche applications.</p></p>
