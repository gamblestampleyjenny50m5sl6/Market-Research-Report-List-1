<p><strong>The Self-lubricated Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metallic Self-lubricated Bearing</li><li>Non-Metallic Self-lubricated Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-lubricated bearings can be categorized into two main types: metallic self-lubricated bearings and non-metallic self-lubricated bearings. Metallic self-lubricated bearings are made of materials such as bronze, brass, or stainless steel with embedded solid lubricants like graphite or PTFE. These bearings are suitable for high load and high temperature applications. On the other hand, non-metallic self-lubricated bearings are typically made of materials like plastic or composite materials with self-lubricating properties. They are often used in applications where corrosion resistance or low friction is required.</p></p>
