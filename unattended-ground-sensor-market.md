<p><p>Unattended Ground Sensor (UGS) market includes different types such as Seismic UGS, Acoustic UGS, Magnetic UGS, Infrared UGS, and Others. Seismic UGS detects ground vibrations caused by footsteps or vehicles. Acoustic UGS detects sound waves for detecting human activity. Magnetic UGS uses changes in the Earth's magnetic field to detect movement. Infrared UGS uses heat signatures to identify targets. Other UGS types may include chemical, biological, or radio frequency sensors for detecting specific threats in a given area.</p></p>
