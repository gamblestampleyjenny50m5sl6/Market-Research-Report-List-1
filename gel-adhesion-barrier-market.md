<p><p>A gel adhesion barrier is a medical device used during surgical procedures to prevent tissue and organ adhesion. It is designed to create a physical barrier between two opposing surfaces to minimize the incidence of post-operative adhesions. These adhesions can occur after surgery when scar tissue forms and binds together nearby tissues or organs, leading to complications such as pain, obstruction, and infertility.</p><p>The global gel adhesion barrier market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Gel Adhesion Barrier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non-absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gel adhesion barrier market includes two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable barriers. Absorbable barriers are made from materials that can be broken down and absorbed by the body over time. They are commonly used in surgeries where shorter-term barrier protection is needed. Non-absorbable barriers, on the other hand, are made from materials that do not break down and are not absorbed by the body. They are often utilized in surgeries where long-term barrier protection is required. Both types play a crucial role in preventing adhesions from occurring after surgery.</p></p>
