<p><strong>The Gearbox for Rail Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Drive</li><li>Transversal Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gearbox for rail vehicles market can be categorized into two main types: Longitudinal Drive and Transversal Drive. Longitudinal Drive gearboxes are positioned along the length of the railcar and are used for vehicles that require high tractive effort. Transversal Drive gearboxes are positioned across the width of the railcar and are suitable for vehicles with lower tractive effort but require better maneuverability. Both types of gearboxes play a crucial role in efficiently transferring power from the motor to the wheels of the rail vehicle.</p></p>
