<p><p>Gold colloid solution market is classified into three types based on solubility: water soluble, oil soluble, and both phase soluble. Water soluble gold colloids are dispersed in water and widely used in biomedical applications. Oil soluble gold colloids are dispersed in oil and commonly used in cosmetics and lubricants. Both phase soluble gold colloids are dispersed in both water and oil, making them versatile for various industries. Each type of gold colloid solution offers unique properties and applications.</p></p>
