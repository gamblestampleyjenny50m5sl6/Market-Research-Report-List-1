<p><ul><li>Pyroelectric Infrared Sensors</li><li>Thermopile Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The human sensor market is categorized into two major types: pyroelectric infrared sensors and thermopile sensors. Pyroelectric infrared sensors detect human presence by measuring changes in infrared radiation emitted by the body. These sensors are commonly used in security systems, motion detectors, and automatic lighting systems. On the other hand, thermopile sensors are designed to detect human presence through changes in temperature. They are utilized in applications such as occupancy monitoring, energy-saving systems, and climate control. Both sensor types play crucial roles in enhancing human safety and optimizing energy consumption in various environments.</p></p>
