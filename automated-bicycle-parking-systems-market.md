<p><p>Automated bicycle parking systems have two main types: semi-automated parking systems and fully automated parking systems. Semi-automated parking systems require some manual intervention from the user, such as placing the bike onto a rack and then activating the system. Fully automated parking systems, on the other hand, handle the entire process automatically from start to finish, with users simply dropping off their bikes at a designated station. Both systems aim to provide convenience and efficiency for bicycle parking in urban areas.</p></p>
