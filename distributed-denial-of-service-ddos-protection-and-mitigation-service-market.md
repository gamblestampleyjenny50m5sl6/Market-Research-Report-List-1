<p><p>Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) Protection and Mitigation services offer various types of defense mechanisms to protect against DDoS attacks. Edge Defense focuses on detecting and mitigating attacks at the network edge. DNS Defense protects against DDoS attacks targeting DNS servers. Cloud Scrubbing Defense involves redirecting traffic to a cloud-based scrubbing center for filtering and cleaning before sending the legitimate traffic back to the network. Other defense mechanisms may include application-layer protection and behavioral analytics to detect and block malicious traffic.</p></p>
