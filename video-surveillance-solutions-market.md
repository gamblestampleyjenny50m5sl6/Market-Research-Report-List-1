<p><p>Video surveillance solutions market can be categorized into two main types: IP Video Surveillance and Analog Video Surveillance. IP Video Surveillance utilizes digital technology to transmit and store video data over an IP network, offering higher resolution, scalability, and advanced features such as remote access and analytics. On the other hand, Analog Video Surveillance relies on traditional analog cameras and DVR systems, which are limited in resolution and feature set compared to IP solutions. Each type caters to different needs and budgets in the surveillance industry.</p></p>
