<p><p>Composite wood is a versatile material used in various markets, including plywood and oriented strand board (OSB). Plywood is made by layering thin sheets of wood veneer together, creating a strong and durable product commonly used in construction. On the other hand, OSB is composed of wood strands bonded together with adhesives, offering a cost-effective alternative to plywood. Both plywood and OSB are widely utilized in the construction industry for applications such as flooring, wall sheathing, and furniture.</p></p>
