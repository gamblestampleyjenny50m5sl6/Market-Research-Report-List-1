<p><strong>The Olefin Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Hollow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Olefin fiber market is divided into two main types: solid and hollow. Solid olefin fibers are continuous and dense, providing strength and durability in various applications such as carpets, upholstery, and apparel. On the other hand, hollow olefin fibers have a tubular structure, which makes them lightweight and breathable, ideal for use in outdoor clothing, filtration systems, and thermal insulation. Both types offer unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
