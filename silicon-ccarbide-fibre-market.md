<p><strong>The Silicon Ccarbide Fibre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Preceramic Polymer Pyrolysis</li><li>Chemical Vapor Reaction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon Carbide Fibre Market includes two main types: Pre-ceramic Polymer Pyrolysis and Chemical Vapor Reaction. Pre-ceramic Polymer Pyrolysis involves transforming a polymer precursor into a ceramic material through controlled heating, resulting in high-performance silicon carbide fibers. On the other hand, Chemical Vapor Reaction method involves the reaction of gaseous silicon and carbon sources at high temperatures to deposit silicon carbide onto a substrate, producing strong and lightweight fibers. Both methods contribute to the growth of the silicon carbide fiber market.</p></p>
