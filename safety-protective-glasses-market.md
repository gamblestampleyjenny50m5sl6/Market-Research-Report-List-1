<p><p>Safety Protective Glasses come in various types, including Absorbent Glasses and Reflective Glasses. Absorbent glasses are designed to absorb harmful UV rays and other high-energy light, providing protection against potential eye damage. On the other hand, Reflective glasses are coated with a special film to reflect light away from the eyes, reducing glare and improving visibility in bright conditions. Both types offer different benefits and cater to specific needs within the safety protective glasses market.</p></p>
