<p><strong>The UVB Filters for Personal Care Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Phase</li><li>Oil Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UVB filters for personal care products are commonly categorized into two main types: water phase and oil phase filters. Water phase filters are hydrophilic and dissolve easily in water-based formulations like lotions and toners. These filters are beneficial for light, non-greasy formulas that are easily absorbed by the skin. On the other hand, oil phase filters are lipophilic and are ideal for oil-based products such as creams and sunscreens. These filters offer better protection against UVB radiation and are suitable for products that require higher SPF levels.</p></p>
