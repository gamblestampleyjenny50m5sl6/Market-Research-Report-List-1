<p><p>KW-Class High Power Fiber Lasers are widely used in various industrial applications, with two main types being Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Lasers and Pulsed Fiber Lasers. Continuous Wave Fiber Lasers operate continuously to provide a steady output of laser light, suitable for cutting, welding, and drilling applications. On the other hand, Pulsed Fiber Lasers emit laser light in pulses, allowing for precise control and higher peak powers, making them ideal for marking, engraving, and micromachining tasks. Both types offer high power outputs and superior performance in their respective applications.</p></p>
