<p><p>A Multilayer Ceramic Substrate (MLCS) is a type of electronic circuit board that plays a crucial role in the development of compact, high-density, and high-performance electronic devices. It is widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, telecommunications, and healthcare, among others.</p><p>MLCS offers several benefits, including high thermal conductivity, excellent electrical insulation, and dimensional stability. These substrates are manufactured by stacking multiple ceramic layers with thin copper traces or conductive paste between them. The process involves sequential layering, printing, and firing of the ceramic materials to form an integrated circuit board.</p><p>The global Multilayer Ceramic Substrate Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for compact and lightweight electronic devices.
