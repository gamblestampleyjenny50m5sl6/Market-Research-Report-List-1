<p><p>The fully compostable toothbrush market consists of toothbrushes that are made entirely of compostable materials, from the bristles to the handle. These toothbrushes can be disposed of in a compost bin where they will naturally break down over time, reducing waste and environmental impact. Both types cater to consumers looking for eco-friendly alternatives in their oral care products.</p></p>
