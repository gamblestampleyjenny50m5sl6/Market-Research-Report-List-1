<p><p>The telecom market encompasses various types, including RF-Based, NB-IoT, and LPWAN. RF-Based (Radio Frequency) technology is a wireless communication method that uses radio waves to transmit data over short distances. NB-IoT (Narrowband Internet of Things) is a low-power wide-area network technology designed for IoT devices that require long battery life and low data rates. LPWAN (Low Power Wide Area Network) is a type of wireless network that enables long-range communication while using minimal power, making it suitable for IoT applications that need to transmit small amounts of data over large distances.</p></p>
