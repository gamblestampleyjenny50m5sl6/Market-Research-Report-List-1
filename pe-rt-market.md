It is commonly available in three market types: Ethylene-Octene, Ethylene-Hexene, and Ethylene-Butene. Each market type refers to the specific blend of ethylene and other copolymers used in the manufacturing process. Ethylene-Octene offers high flexibility, Ethylene-Hexene provides improved processability, and Ethylene-Butene offers enhanced high-temperature performance. These market types cater to different needs and preferences in the plumbing industry.</p></p>
