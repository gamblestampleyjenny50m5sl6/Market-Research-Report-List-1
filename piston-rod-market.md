<p><p>The piston rod market consists of various types of pistons, including differential, cylindrical, and disc pistons. Differential pistons have varying diameters along their length, allowing for different amounts of pressure to be exerted at different points. Cylindrical pistons have a uniform diameter along their length and are commonly used in hydraulic systems. Disc pistons have a flat, disc-shaped design and are primarily used in brake systems. Each type of piston offers unique advantages and is suited for specific applications within the market.</p></p>
