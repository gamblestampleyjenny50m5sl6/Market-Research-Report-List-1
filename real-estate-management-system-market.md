<p><p>Real estate management systems are available in two market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises systems require the software to be installed and hosted on the client's own servers, allowing for greater control and customization. In contrast, cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. These systems offer flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from any location or device. Both market types provide essential tools for property management, such as lease management, financial reporting, and tenant communication, but differ in terms of deployment and infrastructure.</p></p>
