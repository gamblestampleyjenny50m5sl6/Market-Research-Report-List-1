<p><p>Field Device Management (FDM) refers to the process of monitoring, maintaining, and managing field devices in industrial plants or facilities. The increasing adoption of industrial automation and digitization across various industries is driving the demand for efficient management of field devices. The integration of these technologies enables real-time monitoring and remote management of field devices, allowing companies to optimize their operational processes. Additionally, the growing use of wireless communication technologies, such as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, is further enhancing the capabilities of FDM systems.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the increasing adoption of predictive maintenance techniques. This proactive approach to maintenance helps in reducing downtime, improving equipment lifespan, and optimizing maintenance schedules.</p><p>In conclusion, the Field Device Management (FDM) market is poised for significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 8.3% during the forecast period.
