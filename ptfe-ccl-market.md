<p><p>PTFE CCL (Polytetrafluoroethylene Copper Clad Laminate) is commonly available in two types: Fibreglass PTFE CCL and Ceramic Filled Fiberglass PTFE CCL. Fibreglass PTFE CCL is known for its high dielectric strength and thermal stability, making it ideal for use in high frequency applications. On the other hand, Ceramic Filled Fiberglass PTFE CCL offers enhanced thermal conductivity and mechanical strength due to the addition of ceramic fillers.
