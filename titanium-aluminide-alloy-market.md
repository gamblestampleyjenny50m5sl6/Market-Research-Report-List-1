<p><p>Titanium aluminide alloy market can be segmented into two main types - Gamma type and Other type. Gamma type titanium aluminide alloys are primarily composed of gamma titanium aluminides, which offer high-temperature strength and creep resistance, making them suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. On the other hand, Other type titanium aluminide alloys consist of a combination of alpha and gamma phases, providing a balance of strength and ductility for use in various industries such as electronics and medical devices. Both types cater to different market needs and applications.</p></p>
