<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>Medium Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-beam sonar technology is categorized into three market types based on the frequency of the sound waves used for underwater mapping and imaging. Low frequency sonar operates between 20-100 kHz and offers long-range coverage with lower resolution. Medium frequency sonar operates between 100-500 kHz and provides a balance between range and resolution. High frequency sonar operates above 500 kHz and offers high-resolution images but with limited range. Each frequency has its own advantages and is used in various applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
