<p><p>Melamine foam is categorized into three main types based on their structure and flexibility: rigid, semi-rigid, and flexible. Rigid melamine foam is dense and firm, ideal for applications requiring durability and support. Semi-rigid melamine foam offers a balance between flexibility and rigidity, suitable for various insulation and cushioning purposes. Flexible melamine foam is soft and pliable, making it versatile for applications where conformability and comfort are key. Each type caters to specific needs in industries such as construction, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
