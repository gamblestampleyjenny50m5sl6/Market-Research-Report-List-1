<p><p>Buckwheat is available in three main market types: Unhulled, Raw, and Roasted. Unhulled buckwheat is still in its outer shell, offering the most nutrients but requiring more processing before consumption. Raw buckwheat has had its outer shell removed, making it easier to cook and eat while still retaining its nutritional value. Roasted buckwheat has been toasted to enhance its flavor and add a nutty taste to dishes. Each market type offers different options for consumers looking to incorporate buckwheat into their diets.</p></p>
