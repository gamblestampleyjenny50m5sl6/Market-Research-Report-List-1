<p><p>Alnico magnets are divided into three main types based on their manufacturing process: cast Alnico, sintered Alnico, and bar Alnico. Cast Alnico magnets are made by pouring molten metal alloys into molds and cooling them to form solid magnets. Sintered Alnico magnets are produced by compacting powdered metal alloys under high pressure and then heating them to create solid magnets. Bar Alnico magnets are typically in a cylindrical or rectangular shape and are commonly used in various industries for their strong magnetic properties.</p></p>
