<p><p>The Disposable Soft Tissue Dissection Device market includes both bipolar and monopolar devices. Bipolar devices use two electrodes to deliver electrical current, allowing for more precise and controlled tissue dissection. Monopolar devices, on the other hand, use one electrode and require a dispersive pad to complete the circuit. Both types of devices offer benefits in terms of safety, efficacy, and ease of use for surgeons performing soft tissue dissections in various medical procedures.</p></p>
