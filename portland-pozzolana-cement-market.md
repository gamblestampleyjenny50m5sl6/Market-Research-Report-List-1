<p><p>Portland Pozzolana Cement comes in two main types: Artificial Pozzolana Cement and Natural Pozzolana Cement. Artificial Pozzolana Cement is made by combining artificial materials like fly ash, silica fumes, and slag with Portland cement. On the other hand, Natural Pozzolana Cement is made by mixing natural materials like volcanic ash with Portland cement. Both types of Pozzolana Cement provide similar benefits such as increased durability, reduced permeability, and improved workability, making them popular choices in construction projects.</p></p>
