<p><p>Polyester Geogrids are used in construction and civil engineering projects for soil reinforcement. Biaxial Tension geogrids have equal strength in both directions, providing stability for projects such as retaining walls and pavement. Uniaxial Tension geogrids have greater strength in one direction, making them suitable for applications like slope stabilization and road construction. Both types of geogrids play a crucial role in enhancing the durability and strength of various structures, ensuring long-term stability and integrity.</p></p>
