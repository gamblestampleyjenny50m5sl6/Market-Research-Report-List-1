<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic Flame Retardant Material</li><li>Inorganic Flame Retardant Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame retardant materials are classified into two main categories: organic and inorganic. Organic flame retardant materials are derived from carbon-based compounds and include substances like brominated flame retardants and phosphorus-based additives. In contrast, inorganic flame retardant materials are made from minerals or metals such as aluminum hydroxide, magnesium hydroxide, and antimony trioxide. Both types of flame retardant materials work by inhibiting or delaying the spread of fire in various applications such as textiles, plastics, and electronics.</p></p>
