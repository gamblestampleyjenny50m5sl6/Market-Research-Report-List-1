<p><p>Automotive exhaust analyzers are devices used to measure the composition of vehicle emissions. There are various types of analyzers including Non-Dispersive Infrared, Flame Ionization Detector, Chemiluminescence Analyser, and Constant Volume Sampler. Non-Dispersive Infrared analyzers use light absorption to detect gases, Flame Ionization Detectors measure hydrocarbons, Chemiluminescence Analyzers detect NOx emissions, and Constant Volume Samplers collect and measure exhaust samples. Each type of analyzer has its own method for accurately measuring and analyzing emissions from vehicles.</p></p>
