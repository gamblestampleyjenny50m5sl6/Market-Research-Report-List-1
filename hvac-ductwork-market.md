<p><p>HVAC ductwork market includes different types of ducts such as sheet metal ducts, flexible non-metallic ducts, and fiberglass ducts. Sheet metal ducts are made of galvanized steel and are commonly used due to their durability and ease of installation. Flexible non-metallic ducts are made of materials like plastic or foil and are more lightweight and versatile. Fiberglass ducts are known for their thermal insulation properties and are often used in commercial buildings. Each type of duct has its own advantages and applications in HVAC systems.</p></p>
