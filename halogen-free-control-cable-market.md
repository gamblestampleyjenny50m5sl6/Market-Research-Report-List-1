<p><p>Halogen-free control cables are cables that do not contain halogen-based materials which release toxic gases when burned. Two common types of halogen-free control cables are LSZH (Low Smoke Zero Halogen) and FRNC (Flame Retardant Non-Corrosive). LSZH cables are designed to produce minimal smoke and no toxic halogen gases in the event of a fire, making them suitable for use in confined spaces. FRNC cables are designed to be flame retardant and non-corrosive, ensuring safety and reliability in industrial settings.</p></p>
