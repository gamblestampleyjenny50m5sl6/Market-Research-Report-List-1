<p><strong>The Q-Switced Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Q-Switched Ruby Laser</li><li>Q-Switched Nd:YAG Laser</li><li>Q-Switched Alexandride Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Q-Switched Lasers Market comprises three primary types: Q-Switched Ruby Laser, Q-Switched Nd:YAG Laser, and Q-Switched Alexandrite Laser. Q-Switched Ruby Lasers utilize a synthetic ruby crystal as the medium and emit red light. Q-Switched Nd:YAG Lasers employ a neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet crystal, producing infrared light. Lastly, Q-Switched Alexandrite Lasers employ the alexandrite crystal as the medium, emitting red and near-infrared light. These lasers find applications in various fields like dermatology, tattoo removal, and aesthetic medicine due to their ability to deliver high-intensity energy in short pulses, aiding in targeted treatment.</p></p>
