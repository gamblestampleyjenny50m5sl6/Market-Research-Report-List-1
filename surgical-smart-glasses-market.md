<p><p>Surgical smart glasses refer to a type of advanced eyewear used by surgeons during surgical procedures. There are two main types of surgical smart glasses based on their connectivity options: wire type and wireless type. </p><p>Wire type surgical smart glasses require a physical connection to a power source or computer system using wires or cables. This ensures a stable and consistent connection but may restrict movement.</p><p>Wireless type surgical smart glasses, on the other hand, are designed to be connected without wires, using technologies like Bluetooth or Wi-Fi. This enables greater freedom of movement for surgeons during procedures but may be subject to potential connectivity issues.</p></p>
