<p><strong>The Automated Container Terminal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-Automated Terminals</li><li>Fully Automated Terminals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-Automated Terminals: These types of container terminals combine both manual and automated processes. They utilize automated equipment such as robotic cranes for loading and unloading containers, but still require human intervention for certain tasks, such as truck or rail operations or the handling of non-standard cargo.</p><p>Fully Automated Terminals: These container terminals rely almost entirely on automated systems and machinery, minimizing the need for human involvement. They utilize advanced technologies like automated stacking cranes, guided vehicles, and intelligent software systems to handle the entire container handling process, from start to finish, without the need for direct human intervention.</p></p>
