<p><p>Mobile Customer Relationship Management (CRM) refers to the use of mobile technology to manage and improve customer relationships. There are two main market types: on-premise and cloud. On-premise CRM involves installing software directly on a company's servers, giving them full control over the system but requiring high upfront costs and maintenance. On the other hand, cloud CRM operates via the internet, where the software is hosted on remote servers. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cheaper costs as the software is accessed through subscription plans and maintained by the service provider.</p></p>
